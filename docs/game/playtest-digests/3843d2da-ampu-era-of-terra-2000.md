## Ted rulings & new proposals (designer-authoritative; numbered)

1. **‚òÖ Cabinet‚Üíenthusiasm REWORK ‚Äî RE-TUNED LIVE (the marquee, sharpens #124).**
   The old "stacking happiness" rule gave **Mods +18** (capped +3) off one
   cabinet ‚Äî Ted & ebrk call it "completely broken ‚Ä¶ allowing happiness to stack
   while not allowing unhappiness to stack is skewing results" (POST 486-488). Ted
   reworks it LIVE to a **three-state "upset / fine / happy" model** (POST 489):
   a faction (by lobby cards) that gets **‚â•1** of what it wants = **no impact**;
   **>1** = **20%/10% chance +1 enthusiasm**; **ZERO** = **20%/10% chance ‚àí1**;
   **one roll per faction, multiple same-ideology factions STACK the impact;
   max ¬±3.** "I'm thinking this is what we'll follow." (Designer-authoritative.)
2. **‚òÖ NEW Era-of-Terror rule ‚Äî same-party-faction appointment FAIRNESS penalty
   (twice-fired live).** "From the Era of Terror on through the future, the
   president must balance appointments for ALL same-party factions equally
   (including their own). Failing = **‚àí500 points per slighted same-party
   faction**" (POST 1280). Fired on **Bush** (‚àí2000 for over-appointing Zagnut)
   AND on **Oprah** (‚àí2000 for over-appointing himself, POST 154 ch27). Distinct
   from the cabinet-diversity penalty.
3. **‚òÖ Cabinet-DIVERSITY penalty ACTIVE natively in Era of Terror (corroborates
   #124 / the historian's chunk-145 `nuke` note).** Ted asked for female/racial-
   minority flags on the Executive tab "It's relevant for Era of Terror cabinet
   appointments, triggering penalties if certain levels aren't met" (POST 428,
   434, 441). So BOTH Era-of-Terror cabinet penalties (diversity + same-party
   fairness) operate together natively in a 2000-start.
4. **‚òÖ Two canonical Ted house rules ‚Äî both now OFFICIAL (POST 91-92).** (a)
   **Nobody is born with Command; all rookies enter at 0 Command; ALL
   Command-gain %s are DOUBLED** to compensate (corroborates #136/#143/#92). (b)
   **Rolling an already-held expertise = NO new expertise** (don't re-roll) ‚Äî so
   pols master a few expertises instead of becoming renaissance men. ebrk
   confirms "both have been adapted into the official rules now" (POST 92).
5. **‚òÖ Meter‚Üíelection map RESOLVED to V's 2-layer model (resolves #18 for modern).**
   Ted reversed his own interpretation to V's canonical intent: top meters = flat
   universal per-ideology modifier (both parties, primary AND general) + separate
   per-party enthusiasm bonus (POST 913-926; see Mechanics above).
6. **‚òÖ 5% death+retirement rule pinned (corroborates #85).** Ted: 1 death max +

   first, then deaths** (POST 462-466, 496). (The 1840 source split it 50/50
