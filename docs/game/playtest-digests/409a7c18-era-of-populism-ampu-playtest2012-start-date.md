  factions** each loaded with archetype pols + era-tuned ideology/interest/lobby
  decks AND concrete seat counts (e.g. B1 = 6 Sen / 51 House / 0 Kingmakers / 2

   vacate-then-refill an office only with the holder's consent came from the

## §4 — Net-new findings (genuinely new vs. the gap log)

1. **Real-pol draft classes are too thin to populate career tracks — surfaces as
   a LIVE problem (corroborates + sharpens #90/#43).** The 2013 draft had only 15
   Dem + 14 GOP rookies (vs. the usual ~100+, POST 381). Players: *"generated
   pols should be happening at this time … the rules say generating pols starts
   the era BEFORE the era of future"* (POST 383, 386) and *"each faction is now
   tasked with populating seven career tracks with two or three new rookies if we
   don't backfill generated pols"* (POST 392); the modern playtest *"every draft
   has been 1 or 2 short of filling up the track"* (POST 384). Same Rule-3.0.18
   finding as `c50d9da7` (#90), but here it manifests as career-track starvation
   from the very first post-boot draft. **Build needs both gates** (per-era year-
   trigger AND dataset-exhaustion fallback) — corroborated for the 2nd time.

2. **Committee-packaging: the RANKING-MEMBER counter-mechanic + chair-add rules
   (NEW detail — see #174).** Verbatim packaging rules (POST 724) go well beyond
   the chair-block/package captured in `c50d9da7` §4 / #8/#9:
   - **Ranking Member (always opposing party) can UNDO a package or repackage one
     of their party's proposals with a majority proposal** under 5 conditions:
     (a) Efficient + a crisis-trait (bookkeeper/geostrategist/cop/domestic-
     warrior) on a crisis bill; (b) higher legis than the chair; (c) Manipulative
     vs a Pliable/Predictable chair; (d) Iron-Fist vs a Passive chair; (e)
     Magician with equal legis.
   - **Chair-add-bill:** 5 legis + Efficient → may add a tax / income-tax /
     tariff to ANY package even off-committee; 5 legis + Magician → may add one
     extra off-topic proposal.
   - Cross-chamber and cross-committee packaging forbidden; **Puritan never votes
     for/against anything (de)scoring their own ideology** (conflict → ignore).

3. **Law-repealability flag + replace-not-repeal classes (NEW — see #175).**
   Designer-ruled (POST 687-688, above): per-law repealable flag; tax/immigration
   laws are non-repealable-but-replaceable; statehood is permanent. This is the
   concrete, designer-authoritative form of the "why doesn't every meter-impacting
   bill carry the right tag" data-tag hole `c50d9da7` §5.5 only gestured at.
