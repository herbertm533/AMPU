## Era span & predicate-gating (era-aware framing)

Per `docs/game/historical-context.md`, Infrastructure is the genre with the **longest unbroken
era reach** in the sweep — it has real content in EVERY band, including the early game (unlike
Environment, which had no pre-Gilded content). This makes era-bands and sequential prereqs essential
at schema-tag time:

- **Founding/Jacksonian/antebellum floor (~1800s–1850s):** turnpikes, McAdam roads (POST 4),
  **Maysville Road**, **The National Road** + extensions, lighthouses, harbor/coastal defenses,
  early canals, B&O RR, 1st transcontinental RR survey/subsidy. → Maps directly onto the historian
  era frame: the **American System / Whig "internal improvements"** pole (`historical-context.md`
  lines 535, 751, 784) vs. the Jacksonian state-rights / strict-construction pole (the Maysville Road
  veto is the era's defining infrastructure-federalism fight). The "Leave all infrastructure to the
  states" row IS that Jacksonian pole in legprop form.
- **Progressive→New Deal band (~1900–1940):** Newlands Reclamation Act (1902), Federal Aid Road Act
  1916 (POST 4), Highway Act of 1921, Hoover Dam (1931), TVA (1933). Flood control, harbor/waterway
  improvements.
- **Mid-20c band:** Interstate Highway System (1956), National 55 mph (1974), St Lawrence Seaway
  enlargement, coast-to-coast telegraph→ later comms.
- **Modern band:** national high-speed internet / broadband grants, electric-grid renewal, federally
  funded HSR + DC–NYC HSR, Amtrak funding (POST 7), light rail / fiberoptic / renewable transit (POST 6).
- **Future band (#206):** nuclear-reactor conversion, Philippines artificial islands (overpopulation),
  sea colonies, hyperloop (POST 6), bullet trains (POST 5), gondola/cable transit (POST 6),
  Moon research center (POST 7), "Moon Amtrak" (POST 8).
- **Predicate-gating (#258) examples implied/written in-thread:**
  - **★ Sequential / chained-build gate (the headline #258 pattern here):** National Road → "various
    bills extending the National Road" (POST 1); HSR-to-NYC → extend-to-Boston (POST 2); turnpike →
    McAdam → modern road → 1916 act → Highway Act (POST 4). Each next segment is gated on the prior
    build existing — a **sequential legprop unlock chain** no current field expresses.
  - **Institution-dependency gate:** "Allow Bank of US to collect revenue for an infrastructure fund"
    (POST 1) is gated on the Bank of US existing (Banking-genre prereq).
  - **Territory/era gates:** Philippines artificial islands (needs Philippines, ~1900s+); St Lawrence
    Seaway (needs the relevant era/Canada relationship); Moon research center / Moon Amtrak (Future band).
