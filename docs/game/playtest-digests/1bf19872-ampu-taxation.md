## Era span & predicate-gating (era-aware framing)

Per `docs/game/historical-context.md`, taxation content is intrinsically era-banded; this makes
era-bands and prereqs essential at schema-tag time. **No-historian-this-batch** — anchors below
are from the historical-context cross-era map + standard US fiscal history:
- **Founding / antebellum floor (~1772–1860):** the **real fiscal world is tariffs + excises,
  almost no direct/income tax.** "Leave taxation to the states / request taxation from states"
  literally models the **Articles of Confederation** revenue regime (§1: "Congress had no taxing
  power and states were near-sovereign"). The **Force Bill (1833)** is a real Jacksonian-era act
  (federal authority to collect the tariff during the Nullification Crisis) — a genuine 1828–56
  / "Era of Manifest Destiny"-band row. "No Permanent Federal Income Tax" reflects that the first
  federal income tax was a temporary **Civil War** measure (1861–72). [inference, standard fiscal
  history — no source ran this batch]
- **Progressive band (~1909–1916):** the **16th Amendment** (ratified 1913) is the constitutional
  enabler of the permanent federal income tax, and the modern **IRS/Bureau of Internal Revenue**
  era follows — exactly the genre's "16th Amendment" + "Empower the IRS" + "Create IRS" rows.
- **Modern band:** payroll tax for **Social Security** (New Deal, 1935+), capital-gains / estate /
  corporate taxes and their raise/lower laws, EITC / Child Tax Credit, flat-tax proposals,
  gas/crude-oil/energy taxes, Wall-Street-transaction tax, sin/drug taxes, land-value tax.
- **Future band (#206):** **sea-colony income tax**, **tax on genetic & cyber modifications** —
  off-world / post-human taxation, the clearest Future-era rows here.
- **Predicate-gated (#258) examples implied inline:** wartime tax laws → war-active gate;
  temporary peacetime income tax → war-inactive gate; sea-colony tax → ocean-colony era/territory
  gate; 16th Amendment / IRS → Progressive-era gate; payroll-for-Soc-Sec → Social-Security-exists
  gate; tax-farm-equipment-for-subsidies → farm-subsidy-program-exists gate. None is written as an
  explicit `Preq:` field — same prereq-field gap as #258.
