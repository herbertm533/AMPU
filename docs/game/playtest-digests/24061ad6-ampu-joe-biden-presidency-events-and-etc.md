## §3. Designer-authoritative rulings (the high-signal section)

### §3a. The "Elderly President Drops Out of Reelection" event (vcczar, POST 20, 28)
The marquee NEW mechanic this thread defines — a **mid-campaign presidential
replacement event** (the Biden-2024 analog):
- **Trigger:** the president must have been **hit by the age-penalty die roll**
  (the existing 70-or-75 age roll). If so → a **50% chance of being pulled from
  the ticket**, AND a **−1 election penalty to the party** if he proceeds with
  reelection. **The −1 goes to the VP even if the pres is pulled** (it reflects the
  disorganization/in-fighting/alleged-cover-up + the unorthodox replacement).
- **Replacement:** vcczar's design (and the player consensus, POST 29-35,
  matthewyoung) = **drop out and ENDORSE the VP** ("VP replaces President on the
  ticket"), NOT an open convention — the Democrats deliberately avoided an 11th-
  hour convention fight. (Community floated reusing **pre-primary / compromise-
  candidate convention rules** if the VP can't/won't step up, POST 9-13, 17, 28 —
  with a pre-12A "designate a successor" fallback for older eras.)
- **Scope (POST 20):** this is an **Era-of-Populism event, NOT a general event** —
  *"if we have this incident happen twice then I'll convert it"* (the same
  twice-before-generalizing rule seen in other threads, e.g. planb POST 135-136
  Gaza humanitarian-crisis).
- **Refined trigger (community, POST 33):** make it hard — president 80+, a
  negative trait (Frail / Easily Overwhelmed / Incoherent), older than the VP,
  Party-Pref neutral-or-against. (vcczar's own version keys it to the age-roll +
  50%.)
- **VP-younger-than-Pres caveat (POST 30-32):** the endorse-VP path assumes the VP
  isn't even more geriatric — needs a guard.

### §3b. Other rulings
- **All other modern presidents are done (POST 47):** only Biden needs this pass;
  more added **after release**.
- **Trump 2nd term deferred (POST 52):** can't tell yet what's textbook-significant;
  wait until ~midterms; most of what he's doing is undoing Biden (repeals don't
  need their own content).
- **Repeals/junk-actions excluded (POST 42-45, 55):** end-of-term + symbolic
  first-week executive actions don't count; a repeal of a prior action doesn't
  need its own action.
- **"Backfire" requirement (POST 3, 7):** the symbolic-declare-expired-amendment
  pres action must be able to backfire, else every president would spam it.
