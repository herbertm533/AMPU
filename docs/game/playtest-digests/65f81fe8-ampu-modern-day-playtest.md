## §3 — ★ The live era-boundary point-banking ritual (chunk-026, corroborates #68/#2)

This is the digest's single most valuable artifact: **the "End of Historical Era" phase run
live at a real 2024 boundary, with the formula printed verbatim** (POST 1871). It matches the
`rep1800` boundary formula almost exactly — strong cross-thread corroboration that #68/#2 is a
real, deterministic pipeline, not a one-off.

**The 6-clause point-banking formula, as executed (POST 1871):**
1. Faction with **most points in the Historical Era → +5** (jnewt).
2. Faction with **most points from the OTHER party → +3** (pman).
3. **2nd-most-points faction, if same party as the winner → +3** (Hestia).
4. **ALL factions in the party that collectively earned the most points → +3 each** (all 4
   Blue factions).
5. **−1 to all party members IF the top party has an allied faction that finished last/next-last**
   (did NOT trigger).
6. **−1 to the top faction IF it has an allied faction that finished last/next-last** (did NOT
   trigger).

→ **Banked totals (Era of Populism):** jnewt 8, Hestia 6, Vicx 3, pman 3, Murrman 3.
→ **"Scores will be reset to zero for the next Era"** (POST 1871) — the non-banked reset, confirming #51's "running Score economy + era-end awards" and #2's "non-banked points reset to 0."

**Other boundary steps run live (the ~12-step pipeline, #68):**

## §4 — ★ Draft-ideology restrictions: ON in 2016, OFF at 2024 (the #171 toggle, demonstrated flipping)

The single cleanest live demonstration of #171 in the KB. In **the same campaign**, the
draft-restriction system is **ON in the Era of Populism and switched OFF in the Era of the
Near Future**:

  ideologies specified on the Main tab" (POST 558); WVProgressive (R1) "can draft only RW Pops

in future/present timelines"): the toggle isn't just "off in 2024," it **flips at the
